Dental Implants

Advanced Dental Implant Solutions

Dental implants are titanium posts that are surgically placed into the jawbone beneath your gums to replace the roots of missing teeth. Once in place, they allow Dr. Bhowmik to mount replacement teeth onto them, providing a foundation for new teeth that look, feel, and function like natural teeth.

Our state-of-the-art dental implant procedures use the latest technology and techniques to ensure optimal results with minimal discomfort and recovery time.

Titanium & Zirconia Options

Choose from biocompatible titanium implants or metal-free zirconia implants based on your specific needs and preferences.

Single & Full-Arch Solutions

Whether you need to replace a single tooth or an entire arch, we offer customized implant solutions to meet your needs.

Computer-Guided Placement

Using advanced 3D imaging and computer-guided technology for precise implant placement and optimal results.

Bone Grafting & Sinus Lifts

Advanced procedures to prepare your jaw for implants if you lack sufficient bone density.

Orthognathic Surgery

Corrective Jaw Surgery

Orthognathic surgery, also known as corrective jaw surgery, is performed to correct skeletal and dental irregularities, improving both function and facial aesthetics. This procedure can address severe malocclusion (improper bite), facial asymmetry, and jaw discrepancies that cannot be corrected with orthodontics alone.

Dr. Bhowmik uses advanced 3D planning technology to ensure precise surgical outcomes and improved quality of life for patients with jaw-related functional and aesthetic concerns.

3D Surgical Planning

Utilizing advanced 3D imaging and virtual surgical planning for precise, predictable results.

Improved Bite Function

Correcting malocclusion to improve chewing, speaking, and overall oral function.

Enhanced Facial Harmony

Addressing facial asymmetry and profile concerns for improved facial aesthetics.

Sleep Apnea Relief

Can help alleviate obstructive sleep apnea by repositioning the jaw to improve airway space.

Facial Trauma

Facial Trauma Management

Facial trauma can result from accidents, sports injuries, interpersonal violence, or falls, leading to fractures of the jaw, cheekbones, eye sockets, and soft tissue injuries. Dr. Bhowmik specializes in the comprehensive management of facial trauma, focusing on both functional restoration and aesthetic outcomes.

Our approach to facial trauma emphasizes minimally invasive techniques whenever possible, with the goal of restoring function while minimizing visible scarring and promoting rapid healing.

Emergency Care Available

Immediate assessment and treatment for acute facial injuries to prevent complications.

Minimally Invasive Techniques

Using endoscopic and other advanced approaches to reduce surgical trauma and recovery time.

Scar Minimization

Advanced suturing techniques and post-operative care to minimize visible scarring.

Comprehensive Rehabilitation

Coordinated care with specialists in physical therapy, speech therapy, and psychological support when needed.

Wisdom Teeth Extraction

Advanced Wisdom Teeth Removal

Wisdom teeth, or third molars, often need to be removed due to impaction, crowding, or potential future complications. Dr. Bhowmik specializes in the safe and comfortable extraction of wisdom teeth, including complex impactions that require surgical intervention.

Our approach focuses on minimizing discomfort during and after the procedure, with various sedation options available to ensure a stress-free experience for patients of all ages.

Sedation Options Available

From local anesthesia to IV sedation, ensuring comfort throughout the procedure based on your needs and preferences.

Preventive Approach

Early assessment and intervention to prevent complications such as impaction, infection, and damage to adjacent teeth.

Rapid Recovery Protocol

Comprehensive post-operative care instructions and follow-up to ensure quick and comfortable healing.

Complex Case Management

Expertise in handling difficult impactions, including those near nerves or sinuses, with minimal risk.
